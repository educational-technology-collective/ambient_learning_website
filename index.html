About

Welcome to our innovative mobile flashcard app, where learning is seamlessly integrated with the power of spaced repetition to maximize students' long-term educational goals. Our app introduces an engaging approach by utilizing spaced repetition, a scientifically proven technique that optimizes memory retention. Through our app, students encounter meticulously crafted flashcards delivered at strategic intervals, ensuring efficient reinforcement of crucial concepts. What sets us apart is our unique methodology â€“ instead of users generating their own flashcards, our app identifies "learning moments" while users engage with course material, both within the app and through our Chrome extension. These pivotal instances trigger the automatic generation of relevant flashcards, which are then seamlessly incorporated into the user's study plan.
